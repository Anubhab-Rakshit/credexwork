# Economics & Unit Economics

## 1. What is a Converted Lead worth to Credex?
A "Lead" is defined as a company that completes an audit, identifies >$500/mo in savings, and books a consultation with the Credex team to purchase credits.

**The Math (Per Lead):**
- **Average Monthly AI Spend (Target):** $4,500 (across API and SaaS seats - based on May 2026 benchmarks).
- **Potential Monthly Savings:** 28% (Credex average discount) = $1,260/mo.
- **Credex Revenue Model:** Credex takes a ~10% platform fee on credit purchases.
- **Monthly Revenue to Credex:** $450 (10% of the $4,500 spend).
- **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV):** Assuming a 24-month retention (high for B2B infrastructure) = **$10,800 LTV.**

**Lead Valuation:** 
If 20% of consultations convert to a purchase, a single "Booked Consultation" is worth **$2,160** (20% of $10,800).

## 3. The Conversion Funnel
To be profitable and sustainable, we aim for the following conversion benchmarks:

1.  **Unique Visitors** → **Audit Completed:** 20% (High, because the "Hook" is immediate savings).
2.  **Audit Completed** → **High-Savings Flagged (>$300/mo):** 40%.
3.  **High-Savings Flagged** → **Consultation Booked:** 15%.
4.  **Consultation Booked** → **Credit Purchase:** 20%.

**Result:** Every 1,000 visitors generates ~2.4 closed customers ($17k+ in LTV).

## 4. The Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months
To reach $1M in Annual Recurring Revenue (Credex platform fees), we need ~$83,333 in monthly fees.

*   **Average Fee per Customer:** $300/mo.
*   **Total Customers Needed:** 278 active companies.
*   **Monthly Customer Acquisition Needed:** ~15-20 new companies per month (accounting for churn).

**What has to be true:**
1.  **Retention:** AI spend must remain a "top-3" line item for startups, ensuring they don't churn from the Credex credit ecosystem.
2.  **Expansion:** As these startups grow from Seed to Series B, their spend (and our 10% fee) must scale. A single "Scale-up" customer spending $50k/mo on APIs covers the revenue of 15 "Seed" customers.
3.  **The "Audit" as a Quarterly Ritual:** We must convert the one-time audit into a "Quarterly Spend Review" email to keep the viral loop alive and re-engage users.
